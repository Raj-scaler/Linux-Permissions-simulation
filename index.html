ACL Bouncer

A nightclub metaphor. Configure an ACL, send visitors to the door, and watch the bouncer walk through the 4-stage evaluation.

setfacl getfacl mask named user/group evaluation order
Sandbox · edit rules, play the animated check  ·  Predict · 7 rounds — ALLOW or DENY before the reveal
Launch simulator
Suggested flow: Run Linux Permissions first to anchor the owner/group/other mental model, then move to ACL Bouncer to show where classic permissions fall short (named users, mixed groups) and how ACLs + mask extend the system.
